"License Plate Hunt" is a responsive web app, that tracks your progress as you try to find a license plate from every state in the united states. It has a clean, simple, fun user interface that's usually played from a smart phone. There's no login, so it's easy to get started. It uses localstorage for persistence. You can start a new game, if you go on a new trip for instance, and look at results of past games.
